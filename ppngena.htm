About the Chart Generator Script

General

This page uses a large JavaScript. It is written, to the best of my knowledge, in conformance with "javascript" specification at its lowest common denominator. In spite of that, it seems to push the limits of some browsers.

If you have experience to share, good or bad, with this script in your browser, please use the "HELP" "ABOUT" menu or THIS SCRIPT to get the browser's name and version number, and mail your experience and browser information to Stan Pope. Your communications are also invited if you're a "JavaScript-Jock" who can tell me why the script is having trouble with those browsers!
